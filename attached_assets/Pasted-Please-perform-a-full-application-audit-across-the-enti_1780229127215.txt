Please perform a full application audit across the entire codebase.

Review every frontend page, component, route, form, modal, table, dashboard, settings area, and backend/API connection. Identify anything incomplete, partially built, inconsistent, missing, broken, or not connected properly.

Specifically check:

1. UI completeness
- Confirm every main feature has a complete UI.
- Confirm all navigation links work.
- Identify dead buttons, placeholder pages, TODOs, mock sections, or unfinished areas.
- Ensure empty states, loading states, error states, and success states exist where needed.

2. CRUD completeness
For every feature where users can create, view, update, delete, archive, approve, manage, or configure records, confirm the full flow exists:
- List/index page
- Create flow
- Detail page
- Edit/update flow
- Delete/archive/deactivate flow where applicable
- Validation, confirmations, and success/error messages
- Proper routing after each action

3. Detail pages
Ensure detail pages exist for any record users need to manage, such as:
- Users/accounts
- Profiles
- Customers/clients
- Projects/jobs/tasks
- Products/services
- Orders/payments/invoices
- Documents/files
- Messages/notifications
- Settings/preferences
- Team members/roles
- Any other app-specific record type

4. Account and settings management
Confirm users can manage their account where applicable:
- Profile/contact information
- Password/security
- Organization or business details
- Team members and permissions
- Billing/subscription
- Notification preferences
- Integrations/connections

5. Backend/API/data connection
- Confirm frontend actions connect to real backend endpoints.
- Identify mock data, hardcoded data, or temporary placeholders.
- Confirm database models support the UI.
- Confirm create/edit/delete actions persist correctly.
- Confirm auth, permissions, and role-based access work properly.

6. Consistency and polish
- Ensure layouts, buttons, spacing, typography, cards, tables, forms, and page structures are consistent.
- Make the app feel complete, professional, and production-ready.

Please return:
- A clear audit report organized by feature/module.
- A list of missing or incomplete items.
- Specific files, routes, components, endpoints, or models that need updates.
- Recommended fixes in priority order.
